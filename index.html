With Snap It Up!, just:

  1. Make list of things you want to buy.
  2. Upload your list to Snap It Up using your phone, tablet, or computer.
  3. Get the best deals anywhere on all of your favourite items each and every single week!

Snap It Up! works with your local grocery stores to get you the best prices for your favourite grocery items, all at the tip of your fingers. In addition, Snap It Up! lets you keep a running shopping list so that you can see value of the items in your cart change week-by-week as new sales come and go.

FAQ

Is Snap It Up! free?
Yes, Snap It Up! is free.
Do I need to sign up?
Yes, you do need to sign up. However, we won't ever sell your data or access to your confidential data with others. Ever.
I'm too busy to shop this week. Does Snap It Up! deliver?
If your shopping list totals to $60 or more, we can ship your order your location for an additional $14.99.
What's the catch?
There is none! Honestly! Snap It Up works grocery and retail stores near you to give you the best currently advertised price.
